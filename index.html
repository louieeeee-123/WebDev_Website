Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

For the shortcakes:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the strawberries

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries
  • 2-3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

For the whipped cream:

  • 1 cup heavy cream (chilled)
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingertips, cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk and vanilla extract. Gradually pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Gently pat the dough into a rectangle about 3/4 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter or the rim of a glass to cut out circles of dough. Place the dough circles onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them a few inches apart.
  6. Bake the shortcakes in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until they are golden brown. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. While the shortcakes are cooling, prepare the strawberries. In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, granulated sugar, lemon juice. Toss gently to coat the strawberries in the sugar. Let them sit for about 15-20 minutes to allow the juices to release.
  8. To make the whipped cream, pour the chilled heavy cream into a mixing bowl. Add the powdered sugar and the vanilla extract. Using an electric mixer, beat the cream on high speed until stiff peaks form.
  9. To assemble the strawberry shortcakes, slice the cooled shortcakes in half horizontally. Spoon a generous amount of macerated strawberries onto the bottom half of each shortcake. Top with a dollop of whipped cream, then place the other half of the shortcake on top.
  10. Garnish the strawberry shortcakes with additional whipped cream and fresh strawberries, if desired. Serve immediately.

MATCHA TIRAMISU

Ingredients

  • 12 ladyfingers (savoiardi)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (cold)
  • 1/4 cup mascarpone
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1-2 tsp honey (optional)
  • Fresh raspberries
  • Extra matcha for dusting

Steps

  1. Matcha Soak: Whisk matcha with warm milk and honey.
  2. Cream Layer: Whip heavy cream. Mix mascarpone, sugar, vanilla. Fold in whipped cream.
  3. Assemble: Dip ladyfingers in matcha. Layer with cream. Repeat.
  4. Finish: Dust with matcha, top with raspberries. Chill 2 hours.

SPICED APPLE CAKE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups chopped apples

Maple Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Grease a bundt pan.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients together.
  3. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, vanilla.
  4. Stir in sour cream, then dry ingredients. Fold in apples.
  5. Bake 40-45 minutes. Cool before glazing.
  6. Whisk glaze ingredients and drizzle over cake.

GRANOLA BARS

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep: Line 8x8 pan with parchment.
  2. Toast: Bake oats, nuts, seeds at 350°F for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat: Simmer honey and butter 1-2 minutes. Add vanilla.
  4. Mix: Combine all ingredients. Press firmly into pan.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate 2 hours until firm.
  6. Cut: Slice into 10-12 bars.

LEMON BLUEBERRY MUFFINS

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 1/4 cups blueberries

Topping: 2 tbsp sugar + 1 tsp lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line muffin tin.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients and zest.
  3. Mix wet ingredients separately.
  4. Combine wet and dry. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill cups 3/4 full. Add topping.
  6. Bake 18-22 minutes. Cool 5-10 minutes.
